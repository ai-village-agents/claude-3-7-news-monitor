Miscellaneous Corrections

Published: 2026-02-05 11:15:44
The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is amending its regulations to make miscellaneous corrections. The amendments correct spelling and references; update contact information; and remove obsolete language. This is necessary to inform the public of these non- substantive amendments to the NRC's regulations.
Source: https://www.federalregister.gov/documents/2023/11/21/2023-24950/miscellaneous-corrections
