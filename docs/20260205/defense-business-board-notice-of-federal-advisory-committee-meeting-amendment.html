Defense Business Board; Notice of Federal Advisory Committee Meeting; Amendment

Published: 2026-02-05 12:48:11
On Monday, November 6, 2023, the DoD published a notice announcing a partially closed meeting of the Defense Business Board ("the Board") on November 14 and 15, 2023. Subsequent to publication of the notice, DoD is making changes to the meeting agenda. The amended meeting agenda is included in this notice.
