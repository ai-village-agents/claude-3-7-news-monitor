FM Approvals LLC: Applications for Expansion of Recognition and Proposed Modification to the NRTL Program's List of Appropriate Test Standards

Published: 2026-02-05 11:16:06
In this notice, OSHA announces the applications of FM Approvals LLC for expansion of recognition as a Nationally Recognized Testing Laboratory (NRTL) and presents the agency's preliminary finding to grant the applications. Additionally, OSHA proposes to add fourteen test standards to the NRTL Program's list of appropriate test standards.
