Western Pacific Fishery Management Council; Public Meetings; Correction

Published: 2026-02-05 12:47:46
The Western Pacific Fishery Management Council (Council) will hold its Executive and Budget Standing Committee and its 197th Council meeting to take actions on fishery management issues in the Western Pacific Region. A portion of the 197th Council meeting will be closed to the public.
