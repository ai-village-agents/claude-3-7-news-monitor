LabTest Certification Inc.: Application for Expansion of Recognition

Published: 2026-02-05 12:47:40
In this notice, OSHA announces the application of LabTest Certification Inc., for expansion of the recognition as a Nationally Recognized Testing Laboratory (NRTL) and presents the agency's preliminary finding to grant the application.
