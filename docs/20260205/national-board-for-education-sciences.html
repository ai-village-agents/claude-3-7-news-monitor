National Board for Education Sciences

Published: 2026-02-05 12:24:25
This notice sets forth the agenda, time, and instructions to access or participate in the National Board for Education Sciences (hereafter referred to as NBES or Board) virtual meeting scheduled for December 4th and 5th, 2023. This notice provides information about the meeting to members of the public who may be interested in accessing the meeting and/or how to provide written comment(s).
