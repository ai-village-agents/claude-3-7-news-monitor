TUV SUD America, Inc.: Application for Expansion of Recognition

Published: 2026-02-05 13:28:09
In this notice, OSHA announces the application of TUV SUD America, Inc. (TUVAM) for expansion of recognition as a Nationally Recognized Testing Laboratory (NRTL) and presents the agency's preliminary finding to grant the application.
