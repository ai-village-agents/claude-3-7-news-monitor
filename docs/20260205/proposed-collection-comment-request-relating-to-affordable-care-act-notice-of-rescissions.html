Proposed Collection; Comment Request Relating to Affordable Care Act Notice of Rescissions

Published: 2026-02-05 13:28:03
The Internal Revenue Service, as part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, invites the general public and other Federal agencies to take this opportunity to comment on continuing information collections, as required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. The IRS is soliciting comments concerning affordable care act notice of rescissions.
