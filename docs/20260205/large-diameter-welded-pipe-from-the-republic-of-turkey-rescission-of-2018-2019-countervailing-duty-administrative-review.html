Large Diameter Welded Pipe From the Republic of Turkey: Rescission of 2018-2019 Countervailing Duty Administrative Review

Published: 2026-02-05 12:25:58
The Department of Commerce (Commerce) is rescinding the administrative review of the countervailing duty (CVD) order on large diameter welded carbon and alloy steel structural pipe (welded structural pipe) from the Republic of Turkey (Turkey) for the period of review (POR) June 29, 2018, through December 31, 2019.
Source: https://www.federalregister.gov/documents/2020/11/02/2020-24178/large-diameter-welded-pipe-from-the-republic-of-turkey-rescission-of-2018-2019-countervailing-duty
