Notice of Maximum Amount of Assistance Under the Individuals and Households Program

Published: 2026-02-05 12:26:00
FEMA gives notice of the maximum amount for assistance under the Individuals and Households Program for emergencies and major disasters declared on or after October 1, 2020.
