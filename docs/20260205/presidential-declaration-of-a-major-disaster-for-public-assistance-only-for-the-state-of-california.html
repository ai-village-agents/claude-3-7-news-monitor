Presidential Declaration of a Major Disaster for Public Assistance Only for the State of California

Published: 2026-02-05 12:47:55
This is a Notice of the Presidential declaration of a major disaster for Public Assistance Only for the State of California (FEMA- 4750-DR), dated 11/21/2023. Incident: Tropical Storm Hilary. Incident Period: 08/19/2023 through 08/21/2023.
