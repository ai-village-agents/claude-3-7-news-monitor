Information Collection: NRC Form 4, Cumulative Occupational Dose History

Published: 2026-02-05 11:16:06
The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has recently submitted a request for renewal of an existing collection of information to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for review. The information collection is entitled, NRC Form 4, "Cumulative Occupational Dose History."
Source: https://www.federalregister.gov/documents/2023/11/21/2023-25755/information-collection-nrc-form-4-cumulative-occupational-dose-history
