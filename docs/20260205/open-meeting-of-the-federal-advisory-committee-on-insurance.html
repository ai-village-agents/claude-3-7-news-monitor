Open Meeting of the Federal Advisory Committee on Insurance

Published: 2026-02-05 12:26:18
This notice announces that the U.S. Department of the Treasury's Federal Advisory Committee on Insurance (FACI) will meet via videoconference on Thursday, December 2, 2021 from 12:30 p.m.-3:00 p.m. Eastern Time. The meeting is open to the public. The FACI provides non- binding recommendation and advice to the Federal Insurance Office (FIO) in the U.S. Department of Treasury.
