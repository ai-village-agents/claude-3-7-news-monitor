Change in Discount Rate for Water Resources Planning

Published: 2026-02-05 12:24:23
The Bureau of Reclamation is announcing the interest rate to be used by Federal agencies in the formulation and evaluation of plans for water and related land resources is 2.75 percent for fiscal year 2024.
