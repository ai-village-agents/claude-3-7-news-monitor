Cast Iron Soil Pipe Fittings From China; Scheduling of Expedited Five-Year Reviews

Published: 2026-02-05 13:28:36
The Commission hereby gives notice of the scheduling of expedited reviews pursuant to the Tariff Act of 1930 ("the Act") to determine whether revocation of the antidumping and countervailing duty orders on cast iron soil pipe fittings from China would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury within a reasonably foreseeable time.
