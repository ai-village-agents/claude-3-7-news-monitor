Commission Meeting

Published: 2026-02-05 12:24:38
The Susquehanna River Basin Commission will conduct its regular business meeting on December 14, 2023 in Corning, New York. Details concerning the matters to be addressed at the business meeting are contained in the Supplementary Information section of this notice. Also the Commission published a document in the Federal Register on October 16, 2023, concerning its public hearing on November 2, 2023, in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania.
