Presidential Declaration of a Major Disaster for Public Assistance Only for the State of Kansas

Published: 2026-02-05 10:59:20
This is a Notice of the Presidential declaration of a major disaster for Public Assistance Only for the State of Kansas (FEMA-4747- DR), dated 10/26/2023. Incident: Severe Storms, Straight-line Winds, Tornadoes, and Flooding. Incident Period: 07/14/2023 through 07/21/2023.
