Establishment of Homeland Security Advisory Council Subcommittee

Published: 2026-02-05 11:15:35
On November 14, 2023, DHS Secretary Alejandro Mayorkas issued a memorandum tasking the HSAC with establishing a subcommittee to provide recommendations to the Department to combat online child sexual exploitation and abuse.
