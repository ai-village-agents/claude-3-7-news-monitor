Notice of Public Meeting of the South Carolina Advisory Committee

Published: 2026-02-05 11:01:21
Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the South Carolina Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a briefing via web- conference on Wednesday, December 16, 2020, at 12:00 p.m. (EST). The purpose of the briefing is to continue its work and hear from advocates and stakeholders for its project on subminimum wages for people with disabilities.
