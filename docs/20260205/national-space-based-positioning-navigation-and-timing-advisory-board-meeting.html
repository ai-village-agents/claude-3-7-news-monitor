National Space-Based Positioning, Navigation and Timing Advisory Board; Meeting

Published: 2026-02-05 13:27:57
In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) announces a meeting of the National Space-Based Positioning, Navigation and Timing (PNT) Advisory Board. This will be the 29th meeting of the PNT Advisory Board.
