Notice of Department of State Public Guidance for the Protecting Europe's Energy Security Act (PEESA)

Published: 2026-02-05 11:29:05
The Department of State is issuing public guidance for PEESA on October 20, 2020 to take an expansive interpretation of the available authorities under PEESA by issuing clarifying public guidance and FAQs on the term "provided those vessels for the construction of such a project" under Section 7503(a)(1)(B) of PEESA.
