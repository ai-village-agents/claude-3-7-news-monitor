Notice of Public Meeting of the Ohio Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

Published: 2026-02-05 13:28:24
Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act, that the Ohio Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will hold a public meeting via Zoom. The purpose of the meeting is to discuss the outline of the draft report on the source of income discrimination in Ohio housing.
