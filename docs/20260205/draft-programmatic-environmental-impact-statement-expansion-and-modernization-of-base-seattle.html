Draft Programmatic Environmental Impact Statement; Expansion and Modernization of Base Seattle

Published: 2026-02-05 12:26:44
The Coast Guard is extending the comment period by two weeks for the Draft Programmatic Environmental Impact Statement (PEIS) for the proposed Expansion and Modernization of Base Seattle. Notice of Availability of the Draft PEIS was published on October 11, 2022 to invite public comments on the Draft PEIS. We are extending the comment period through December 16, 2022.
