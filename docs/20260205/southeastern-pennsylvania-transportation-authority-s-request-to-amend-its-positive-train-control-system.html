Southeastern Pennsylvania Transportation Authority's Request To Amend Its Positive Train Control System

Published: 2026-02-05 12:25:50
This document provides the public with notice that, on October 20, 2023, the Southeastern Pennsylvania Transportation Authority (SEPTA) submitted a request for amendment (RFA) to its FRA-certified positive train control (PTC) system. FRA is publishing this notice and inviting public comment on the railroad's RFA to its PTC system.
