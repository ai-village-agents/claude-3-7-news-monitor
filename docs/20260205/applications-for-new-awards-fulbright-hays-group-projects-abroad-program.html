Applications for New Awards; Fulbright-Hays Group Projects Abroad Program

Published: 2026-02-05 11:14:34
The Department of Education is issuing a notice inviting applications for fiscal year (FY) 2022 for the Fulbright-Hays Group Projects Abroad (GPA) Program, Assistance Listing Numbers 84.021A and 84.021B. This notice relates to the approved information collection under OMB control number 1840-0792.
