Notice of Adjustment of Minimum Project Worksheet Amount

Published: 2026-02-05 12:25:57
FEMA gives notice that the minimum Project Worksheet Amount under the Public Assistance program for disasters and emergencies declared on or after October 1, 2020, will be increased.
