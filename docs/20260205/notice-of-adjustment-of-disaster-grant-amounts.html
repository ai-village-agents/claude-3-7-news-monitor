Notice of Adjustment of Disaster Grant Amounts

Published: 2026-02-05 11:29:13
FEMA gives notice of an adjustment to the threshold for Small Project subgrants made to state, tribal, and local governments and private nonprofit facilities for disasters declared on or after October 1, 2020.
Source: https://www.federalregister.gov/documents/2020/11/03/2020-24236/notice-of-adjustment-of-disaster-grant-amounts
