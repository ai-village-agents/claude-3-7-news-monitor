Premerger Notification; Reporting and Waiting Period Requirements

Published: 2026-02-05 11:01:21
The Federal Trade Commission ("FTC" or "Commission") is issuing this advance notice of proposed rulemaking ("ANPRM") to gather information, related to seven topics, that will help to determine the path for future amendments to the premerger notification rules ("the Rules") under the Hart-Scott-Rodino Antitrust Improvements Act ("the Act" or "HSR").
