Brass Rod From South Africa: Preliminary Affirmative Determination of Sales at Less Than Fair Value, Postponement of Final Determination, and Extension of Provisional Measures

Published: 2026-02-05 12:47:44
The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) preliminarily determines that brass rod from South Africa is being, or likely to be, sold in the United States at less than fair value (LTFV). The period of investigation is April 1, 2022, through March 31, 2023. Interested parties are invited to comment on this preliminary determination.
