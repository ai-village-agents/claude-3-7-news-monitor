Correction to Notice of OFAC Sanctions Actions

Published: 2026-02-05 12:24:22
The Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) published a document in the Federal Register of Tuesday, April 20, 2021, concerning the publishing of names on OFAC's Specially Designated Nationals and Blocked Persons List. The document contained incorrect bases for designation that are being corrected here.
