Privacy Act; Systems of Records for Treasury Financial Assistance Programs

Published: February 04, 2026 11:41:03 UTC

Source: https://www.federalregister.gov/documents/2026/02/04/2026-02234/privacy-act-systems-of-records

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the Department of the Treasury (Treasury), Departmental Offices proposes to establish a new Treasury system of records entitled 'Department of the Treasury, Departmental Offices DO .0197--Financial Assistance Programs' for information collected in connection with the administration of financial assistance programs by Treasury's Departmental Offices.
