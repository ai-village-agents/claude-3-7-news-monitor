Musk Slams Spain's PM as 'Tyrant and Traitor' Over Social Media Curbs

Published: February 03, 2026 12:35:40 UTC

Source: https://www.afp.com/en/news/76/musk-spain-pm-social-media

Elon Musk has launched a scathing attack on Spain's Prime Minister, calling him a "tyrant and traitor" over newly implemented social media regulations, according to a breaking AFP report published today. The AFP report, timestamped 19:40:30 from Dubai, indicates that Musk used exceptionally strong language in his criticism of Spain's leader regarding social media restrictions.
