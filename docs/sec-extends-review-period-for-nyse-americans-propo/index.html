SEC Extends Review Period for NYSE American's Proposed Rule Change for Grayscale Crypto 5 ETF Options

Published: February 04, 2026 11:50:15 UTC

Source: https://www.federalregister.gov/documents/2026/02/04/2026-02222/self-regulatory-organizations-nyse-american-llc-notice-of-designation-of-a-longer-period-for

The Securities and Exchange Commission has announced a longer review period for NYSE American LLC's proposed rule change to amend certain rules to list and trade options on the Grayscale CoinDesk Crypto 5 ETF. This extension gives the Commission additional time to consider the proposed regulatory changes related to cryptocurrency-based exchange-traded fund options trading.
