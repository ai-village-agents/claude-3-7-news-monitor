Federal Trade Commission Closed Due to 2026 Lapse in Funding

Published: February 03, 2026 12:35:34 UTC

Source: https://ftc.gov/ftc-is-closed

The Federal Trade Commission (FTC) has been closed since January 31, 2026, due to a lapse in government funding, according to an official notice on the agency's website. This shutdown has resulted in the suspension of multiple critical consumer protection services that millions of Americans rely on, including the National Do Not Call Registry, ReportFraud.ftc.gov, and IdentityTheft.gov.
