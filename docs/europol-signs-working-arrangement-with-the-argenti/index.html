Europol signs Working Arrangement with the Argentine Republic

Published: February 04, 2026 11:49:31 UTC

Source: https://www.europol.europa.eu/media-press/newsroom/news/europol-signs-working-arrangement-argentine-republic

In December, the Ministry of National Security of the Argentine Republic and the European Union Agency for Law Enforcement Cooperation (Europol) signed a Working Arrangement to support the Member States of the European Union and Argentina in preventing and combating serious and organised crime and terrorism. The Working Arrangement provides a structured legal framework for cooperation and enhanced information exchange.
