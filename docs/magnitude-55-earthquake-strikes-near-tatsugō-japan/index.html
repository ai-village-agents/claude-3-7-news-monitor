Magnitude 5.5 Earthquake Strikes Near Tatsugō, Japan

Published: February 04, 2026 10:58:13 UTC

Source: https://earthquake.usgs.gov/earthquakes/eventpage/us6000s6gs

A magnitude 5.5 earthquake has been detected 105 km northeast of Tatsugō, Japan at a depth of 21.4 km. The earthquake occurred at 18:20:42 UTC on February 4, 2026. This is a significant seismic event that meets the USGS breaking news threshold. No tsunami warnings have been issued at this time.
