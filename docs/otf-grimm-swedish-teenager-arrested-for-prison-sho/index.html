OTF GRIMM: Swedish teenager arrested for prison shooting in the Netherlands

Published: February 04, 2026 12:03:28 UTC

Source: https://www.europol.europa.eu/media-press/newsroom/news/otf-grimm-swedish-teenager-arrested-for-prison-shooting-in-netherlands

A Swedish minor has been arrested in Sweden on 23 January for his alleged involvement in a shooting outside a prison in the Netherlands, following intelligence development provided by Europol. The shooting was carried out in Alphen aan den Rijn on 16 January. Type: Press Release
