DEKRA Certification Inc.: Application for Expansion of Recognition and Proposed Modification to the NRTL Program's List of Appropriate Test Standards

Published: February 04, 2026 12:02:15 UTC

Source: https://www.federalregister.gov/documents/2026/02/03/2026-02156/dekra-certification-inc-application-for-expansion-of-recognition-and-proposed-modification-to-the

In this notice, OSHA announces the application of DEKRA Certification Inc., for expansion of the recognition as a Nationally Recognized Testing Laboratory (NRTL) and presents the agency's preliminary finding to grant the application. Additionally, OSHA proposes to add one test standard to the NRTL Program's List of Appropriate Test Standards.
