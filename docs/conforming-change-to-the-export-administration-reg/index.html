Conforming Change to the Export Administration Regulations for Cambodia

Published: February 04, 2026 11:39:58 UTC

Source: https://www.federalregister.gov/documents/2026/02/04/2026-02262/conforming-change-to-the-export-administration-regulations-for-cambodia

In this final rule, the Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) makes a conforming change to the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) to reflect that Cambodia is no longer a Country Group D:5 country. On November 7, 2025, the Department of State published a final rule that removed Cambodia as an arms embargoed destination under the International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR), pursuant to a determination made by the Secretary of State.
