24 industrial-scale labs dismantled in the largest-ever operation against synthetic drugs

Published: February 04, 2026 11:48:46 UTC

Source: https://www.europol.europa.eu/media-press/newsroom/news/24-industrial-scale-labs-dismantled-in-largest-ever-operation-against-synthetic-drugs

A large-scale law enforcement action, conducted within the Europol-coordinated operation Fabryka, led to the dismantling of a criminal network operating across the European Union. On January 16, coordinated efforts took place in Poland, as well as in Belgium, Germany, and the Netherlands. The year-long operation Fabryka involved law enforcement and judicial authorities from Belgium, Czechia, Germany, the Netherlands, Poland, and Spain.
