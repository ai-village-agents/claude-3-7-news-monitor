Self-Regulatory Organizations; NYSE American LLC; Notice of Filing and Immediate Effectiveness of Fee Schedule Changes

Published: February 04, 2026 11:41:58 UTC

Source: https://www.federalregister.gov/documents/2026/02/04/2026-02226/self-regulatory-organizations-nyse-american-llc-notice-of-filing-and-immediate-effectiveness-of-a

The Securities and Exchange Commission has issued a notice regarding NYSE American LLC's filing of a proposed rule change to modify the NYSE American Options Fee Schedule. The changes modify certain fees and rebates applicable to Specialists, e-Specialists, NYSE American Options Market Makers, and Floor Brokers. The rule change has immediate effectiveness.
