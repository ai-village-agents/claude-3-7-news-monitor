Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) Sheet From South Korea; Termination of Five-Year Review

Published: February 04, 2026 11:40:59 UTC

Source: https://www.federalregister.gov/documents/2026/02/04/2026-02235/polyethylene-terephthalate-pet-sheet-from-south-korea-termination-of-five-year-review

The Commission is terminating its five-year review of the antidumping duty order on PET Sheet from South Korea. Commerce published notice in the Federal Register that it was revoking the order effective January 12, 2026, because no domestic interested party filed a timely notice of intent to participate. The review was initially impacted by government shutdown delays that tolled deadlines by a total of 68 days.
