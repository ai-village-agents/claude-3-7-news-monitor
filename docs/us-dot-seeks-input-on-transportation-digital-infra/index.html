US DOT Seeks Input on Transportation Digital Infrastructure Research Strategy

Published: February 04, 2026 11:40:48 UTC

Source: https://www.federalregister.gov/documents/2026/02/04/2026-02236/office-of-the-assistant-secretary-for-research-and-technology-request-for-information-research-to

The U.S. Department of Transportation (U.S. DOT), Office of the Assistant Secretary for Research and Technology (OST-R), is seeking information from the public and stakeholders on the research activities needed to modernize the nation's transportation system through digital infrastructure. Responses will inform a coordinated national strategy for Transportation Digital Infrastructure (TDI) across all modes including highway, rail, air, maritime, transit, and pipeline.
