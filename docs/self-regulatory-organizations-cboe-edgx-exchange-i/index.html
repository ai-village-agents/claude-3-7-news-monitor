Self-Regulatory Organizations; Cboe EDGX Exchange, Inc.; Notice of Filing and Immediate Effectiveness of a Proposed Rule Change To Revise EdgeRisk Self Trade Prevention Modifiers

Published: February 04, 2026 11:41:50 UTC

Source: https://www.federalregister.gov/documents/2026/02/04/2026-02227/self-regulatory-organizations-cboe-edgx-exchange-inc-notice-of-filing-and-immediate-effectiveness-of

The Securities and Exchange Commission has issued a notice regarding Cboe EDGX Exchange's filing of a proposed rule change to amend Exchange Rule 11.10(d) ('EdgeRisk Self Trade Prevention (ERSTP) Modifiers') to revise the definition of Unique Identifier. The rule change has immediate effectiveness.
